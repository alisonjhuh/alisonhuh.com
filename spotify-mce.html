putting users listeners first

i've been a classical musician for three-quarters of my life and through years of performing, i've always learned to tailor my performances to my audience. whether it was a competition judge or grandpa at a family dinner, i constantly strived to help others discover the same joy i felt towards the music i wanted to share with them.

that audience-first mentality carried over into my non-musical endeavors as i explored marketing and design in my college career. working in these roles with a range of audiences, spanning across symphony orchestras, university departments, and the hackathon space, i began to pick up a few new skills for each industry as i learned how to quantifiably measure that user, or audience, experience for evaluation, iteration, and improvement.
