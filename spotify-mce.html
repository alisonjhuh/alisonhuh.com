Users Listeners First

My past work in marketing has always taught me to listen to my audience first. When it comes to my playlist curation that means making the most seamless listening experience.

So what does that really mean?

Take a listen to my "a moment apart playlist" (no shuffle). I've designed this playlist so that every song's last note belong in a major or diminished 7th chord of the next song's first notes. When it comes to playlist organization, every detail counts.

An Idea I'd Love to Bring to Life

Synesthesia (n): a perceptual phenomenon in which stimulation of one sensory or cognitive pathway leads to involuntary experiences in a second sensory or cognitive pathway.

Ever since I read about synesthesia in my literature and psychology class, I've never been able to stop thinking about how we as individual listeners associate certain songs and sounds with different sensations. What better way to obsess over something as abstract as synesthesia than with a playlist collection!
