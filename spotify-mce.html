Building Communities,
One Playlist at a Time.

Keeping up with the trends, I've definitely hopped on the collaborative playlist train during quarantine.

Plus, there's nothing that's as funny-yet-sad as seeing someone else awake at 4am putting a song on the playlist during the late-night hours of a hackathon.
