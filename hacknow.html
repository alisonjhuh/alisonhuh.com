description
hack:now was a 36-hour virtual hackathon co-hosted by cal hacks and postman to empower hackers to tackle the various challenges faced during the covid-19 pandemic. i led the team's marketing efforts, managing cal hacks' social media presence, sending logistical and sponsored emails, collaborating with our event partners such as y combinator and postman for cross-channel promotion, and jumpstarting a referral campaign to boost our sign-up numbers by 203 registrants. i also worked with our tech and design teams to write our event site's web copy.

by the end of the 3 week promotion period, we registered 2,558 hackers for our event.
