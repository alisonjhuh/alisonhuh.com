description
hack:now was a 36-hour virtual hackathon co-hosted by cal hacks and postman to empower hackers to tackle the various challenges faced during the covid-19 pandemic. i led the team's marketing efforts, managing our social media presence, sending logistical & sponsored emails, and collaborating with our event partners such as y combinator & postman for cross-channel promotion. i also worked with our tech and design teams to write our event site's web copy!

by the end of the 3 week promotion period, we registered 2,558 hackers for our event.
